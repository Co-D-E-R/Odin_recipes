!!!DESSERT RECIPES!!!

Use fresh or frozen dark cherries to make this sweet and tart-flavored ice cream that everyone will love. For an extra sweet addition, toss in some chocolate chips. If you can't find cherry juice concentrate, reviewers suggest using grenadine.

    • ¼ cup cherry juice concentrate
      ½ cup fat free milk
      1 cup vanilla low-fat yogurt
      1 cup heavy cream
      ½ cup white sugar
      1 pinch salt
      1 cup frozen dark sweet cherries
      2 teaspoons almond extract
    • Step 1:Place the cherry juice, milk, yogurt, and heavy cream into the bowl of a blender. Add the sugar, salt, cherries, and almond extract. Puree until only small bits of the cherries remain.
      Step 2:Pour into a 1 1/2 quart ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's directions.
